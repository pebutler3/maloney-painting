About Us


Maloney Painting and Decorating has been in business in Colorado for over 30 years. We are family owned and operated. We are unique in that we maintain clean and sober paint crews. None of our employees drink alcohol or use drugs. This is a rarity in the painting industry and we are proud to offer this promise.

In 2014, original owner Mike Maloney retired and passed his knowledge and expertise in the painting business to his eldest son, Greg. We are proud to carry on the legacy Mike has created as a paint contractor; ensuring quality, craftsmanship and pride in doing a better job for each job.
